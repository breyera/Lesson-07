BIRTHDAYS • CORPORATE TEAM BUILDING • “JUST FOR FUN” EVENTS

Bring your club, scout troop, friends and colleagues to Straight to the Point Archery for a fun-filled session on your own private archery range. Our coaching professionals will provide fun and entertaining archery instruction.

Our economical group and party packages include equipment rentals, personal instruction from our team of certified coaches, and use of our lobby space for refreshments or pre-/post- archery gatherings.

Birthday Parties
Have your next birthday party at Straight to the Point Archery! Enjoy the use of our indoor range with archery games, casual competition, novelty shots and prizes. We have a party-room with tables and chairs to accommodate your birthday party. We will hold a mini archery class and will provide all the necessary equipment to help everyone learn and get shooting. Each party will be about 2 hours long and packed with fun! Reservations are required and there is a 10 archer minimum. $35.00 per archer.
Corporate Events
Hold your next corporate party at Straight to the Point Archery. We provide a fun way to build team spirit and camaraderie! Use our “event” room to have a team meeting before getting out to the lanes to shoot. We will hold a mini archery class and will provide all the necessary equipment to get your team shooting. Each event is 2 hours long. Get your peers together and enjoy team building challenges. Reservations are required and there is a 10 archer minimum. $35.00 per archer.
Scout Training
Earn the Archery merit badge or just come to have fun! Straight to the Point Archery is a verified merit badge counselor. We cover badge requirements in a clear, engaging way that helps to instill a strong understanding and appreciation for archery.
